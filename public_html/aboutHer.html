Likely to be found indoors with close friends watching TV. Rarely found in pubs partying with strangers. And if you find her in any such social gathering, You'll notice 3 things -

  1. She'd be mute.
  2. She'd have her fake smile on.
  3. Nobody would be fooled by that because she's not good at fake smiles.
Loves her family to death.
